What to Expect

Your first visit guide

Home/Worship/What to Expect

We know visiting a new church can feel uncertain. Here's what you can expect when you join us.

1 When You Arrive

You'll find our greeters at the doors ready to welcome you and help you find your way. Feel free to arrive a few minutes early to get settled. Parking is free and accessible spaces are available near the main entrance.

2 What to Wear

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans to dress clothes. We care more about your presence than your outfit. Just wear whatever makes you comfortable.

3 The Worship Service

Our services typically last about an hour. We sing a blend of traditional hymns and contemporary songs, hear a message from the Bible, and have time for prayer. Don't worry if you don't know the songs - lyrics are displayed on screen.

Authentic, heartfelt worship

4 For Your Kids

We have safe, fun programs for children of all ages during the service. Our volunteers are background-checked and trained. Just stop by our check-in area and we'll get your kids set up!

All ages and backgrounds welcome

Everyone Is Welcome

At Delmar Church of Nazarene, you'll find people from all walks of life and backgrounds. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time, returning after time away, or looking for a new church home, you belong here.
