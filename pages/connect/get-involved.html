Everyone Has a Place

God has given each of us unique gifts and talents. We believe that everyone can make a difference by serving others. Discover how you can get involved!

Children's Ministry

Help our kids learn about Jesus through teaching, leading activities, and providing a safe environment.

Greeter Team

Welcome guests and help people feel at home when they visit our church.

Outreach

Serve our community through local missions, food drives, and compassionate ministry projects.

Media Team

Help with sound, video, livestreaming, and projection during services and events.
