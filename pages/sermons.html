Weekly Biblical Teaching

God's Word for Your Life

At Delmar Church of the Nazarene, we believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our guide for life. Each week, Pastor Charles Leroy brings relevant, Bible-based messages designed to help you grow in your faith, understand God's plan for your life, and equip you to live as a follower of Jesus Christ. Whether you're new to the Bible or have been studying for years, our sermons aim to meet you where you are and help you take the next step in your spiritual journey.
