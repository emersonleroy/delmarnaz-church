Mission & Vision

Why we exist and where we're going

Home/About/Mission & Vision

Our Mission

"To make Christlike disciples in the nations."

This is the mission of the Church of the Nazarene. At Delmar Nazarene, we live out this mission by loving God, loving people, and serving our community with the transforming love of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

"A community transformed by grace, transforming our world with love."

We envision a church where every person experiences the life-changing grace of God and is empowered to share that love with othersâ€”in our neighborhood and around the world.

Our Core Values

These values shape everything we do as a church family.

Love God

We pursue a passionate relationship with God through worship, prayer, and the study of His Word.

Love People

We welcome everyone with open arms, building authentic relationships in Christian community.

Serve the World

We actively serve our neighbors and support global missions to share God's love everywhere.

Grow in Faith

We are committed to lifelong spiritual growth and helping others discover their calling.

Build Community

We foster deep, meaningful connections through small groups, fellowship, and shared experiences.

Live with Integrity

We pursue holiness in all areas of life, reflecting Christ's character in our words and actions.

Join Us on the Journey

We're better together. Whether you're just exploring faith or looking to grow deeper, there's a place for you here.
