Our Story

A church family united by faith, hope, and love

Home / About / Our Story

Our Mission

To lead people to Jesus Christ, nurture them in faith, and equip them to serve God and others.

We are committed to:

Spreading the Gospel throughout our community through evangelism and compassionate service

Making disciples who love God and others wholeheartedly

Worshiping God in spirit and truth, in the language of our hearts

Supporting families in growing together in faith

Creating a space where generations can worship God together

Our Vision

"To be a Christ-centered, Spirit-led, and community-focused church where lives are transformed, families are strengthened, and hope is shared locally and globally."
